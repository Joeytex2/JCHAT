JCHAT Community Vibe Guide

Welcome to JCHAT! ‚ú® Our community thrives on good vibes and mutual respect. This guide is all about helping you create a safe, fun, and inclusive space for everyone. It's not a list of boring rules‚Äîit's a guide to being awesome!

1. Be a Good Human ü§ù

We're all here to connect and have a good time. That means:

  • Be Kind, Always: Treat others with respect, even if you disagree. Remember there's a real person with feelings on the other side of the screen.
  • No Hate Speech or Harassment: This is a zero-tolerance policy. Do not post or share anything that is discriminatory, racist, sexist, homophobic, or targets individuals or groups.
  • Keep It Positive: Avoid personal attacks, insults, and demeaning comments. Let's lift each other up, not tear each other down.
  • No Impersonation: Do not pretend to be another person, a JCHAT staff member, or a public figure to deceive or mislead others.
  • Respect Intellectual Property: Do not post content that infringes on copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights. Always give credit where it's due.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Be mindful of cultural differences and avoid making jokes or comments that could be offensive or misunderstood by others.
  • Constructive Criticism: If you must criticize, do so constructively. Focus on the content, not the person, and offer helpful suggestions rather than just negativity.
  • Embrace Diversity: JCHAT is a global community. Celebrate the different backgrounds, opinions, and experiences of all users.

2. Respect Privacy üîí

Just like in real life, privacy is super important here. Don't share information that isn't yours to share.

  • Protect Your Info: Don't share your own personal details like phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.
  • Respect Others' Privacy: Do not post private conversations, photos, or videos of others without their explicit permission.
  • Think Before You Post: Once something is online, it's hard to take back. Be mindful of what you share and how it could affect others.
  • No Doxing: Sharing private or identifying information about another user without their consent is strictly prohibited.
  • Maintain Confidentiality: Do not share sensitive information about JCHAT's internal workings or features that have not been publicly released.
  • Private Messages are Private: Don't screenshot or share private messages in public chats without the consent of all parties involved.
  • Secure Your Account: Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication if available to protect your personal information.
  • Be Cautious of Links: Don't click on suspicious links or download files from people you don't know.

3. Keep It Clean (ish) üòâ

Our platform is for a broad audience. Let's keep the content friendly and appropriate for all members.

  • No NSFW Content: Posting sexually explicit, excessively violent, or graphic content is not allowed.
  • No Shocking Content: Avoid gore, disturbing imagery, or anything designed purely to shock or disgust other users.
  • No Encouraging Harm: Do not post content that encourages or glorifies self-harm, violence, or dangerous illegal acts.
  • No Unsolicited Advertising: Do not post links or content that promotes gambling, illegal goods, or other inappropriate commercial activities.
  • Respect Minors: JCHAT is not a place for adult-themed conversations with minors. Protect children by not sharing inappropriate content with them.
  • Substance Abuse: Do not post content that promotes or glorifies illegal drug use or dangerous substance abuse.
  • No Self-Promotion in Public Chats: While it's okay to share your projects in relevant spaces, avoid excessive self-promotion in public, general chats.
  • Be Mindful of Language: While we don't have a strict profanity filter, using excessive profanity can make others uncomfortable. Please be considerate.

4. Don't Be a Spammer or Scammer üé£

Spam and scams ruin the fun for everyone. Let's keep the conversations real and genuine.

  • No Spamming: Don't post repetitive, unsolicited, or promotional content. We want to hear from you, not a bot!
  • No Scamming: Phishing, scams, or any deceptive behavior to get personal information or money from other users is strictly forbidden.
  • No Unauthorized Software: Do not use or promote any automated scripts, bots, or third-party software that interferes with the normal operation of JCHAT.
  • No Trolling: Do not intentionally post inflammatory, irrelevant, or off-topic content to provoke others.
  • No Mass DMing: Sending unsolicited, identical messages to a large number of users is a form of spam and is not allowed.
  • No False Information: Do not spread misinformation or fake news, especially if it could cause harm or confusion.
  • Use Channels Correctly: Post your content in the correct channels or chats. For example, don't post memes in a technical support channel.
  • Avoid Flooding: Do not flood a chat with a large number of messages in a short period of time. It disrupts the conversation for everyone.

You're responsible for your own content. Please make sure everything you share is legal and ethical.

  • Stay Legal: Do not post or promote illegal activities or content. This includes, but is not limited to, content related to illegal drugs, weapons, or terrorism.
  • Abide by Age Restrictions: If a chat or feature has age restrictions, please respect them. Do not try to bypass them or encourage others to do so.
  • Report Illegal Content: If you encounter content that is illegal, you have a duty to report it immediately to our moderation team.
  • No Malicious Activity: Do not engage in any activity that could harm JCHAT's servers, other users' accounts, or the overall integrity of the platform.

If you see something that doesn't feel right, you can help! You are a key part of keeping JCHAT a great place.

  • Report, Don't Retaliate: If you see a violation, use the reporting feature. Don't engage with the user or try to handle it yourself. Our moderation team will take it from there!
  • Provide Clear Information: When you report a user or post, provide as much detail as possible, including screenshots or links, to help our team investigate effectively.
